# Setup Guide for LG QNED85 Series TVs

Type: Retail  
Year: 2024  
Compatibility: Good  

## Pre-requisites

- TV at setup stage (may require factory reset if previously configured)
- TV Manufacturer Remote control
- Apple TV Connected to HDMI1

## Setup Steps

1. Skip network setup on initial prompts.
1. User Environment Analysis - Way to Watch TV: NONE
1. Broadcast Address - Enter ZIP
1. Device Installation Type - Wall mounted.
1. All AI settings disabled: Off by default!
1. Always Ready: Turn Off
1. Settings/General/Energy Saving - Energy Savings Step - Off
1. Settings/General/System/Additional Settings -Quickstart - On by default
1. Settings/General/System/Additional Settings/Screen Saver Promotion - Off
1. Settings/General/System/Additional Settings/Home Settings - Power On Screen - Recent Input
1. Settings/General/System/Additional Settings/Home Settings - Home Promotion - Off 
1. Settings/General/System/Additional/Home Settings - Content Recommendation - Off
1. Settings/General/External Devices/HDMI Settings - Simplink (HDMI CEC) On by Default
1. Settings/General/External Devices - Universal Controller Settings - Select HDMI 1 (Apple OTT/ Manage Settings/ Delete Settings - Yes to reset Universal Controller Settings
1. Settings/General/External Devices - TV On with Mobile - Off BY DEFAULT!

## Testing Steps
1. Power TV off
1. Power TV back on
1. Confirm TV power on To HDMI1 and the AppleTV is displayed.
1. Confirm no on screen menus are displayed (There may be an input banner at the top of the screen for a few seconds)
1. Confirm TV navigation buttons allow navigation of the TV interface. Buttons to test: Up, Left, Right, Down, Select/OK and Back.
