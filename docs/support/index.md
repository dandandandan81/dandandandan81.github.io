# Support Escalation & Fault Reporting

ROOMNET offers the following options for all support issues, incident reporting, scheduled repair and maintenance requests, general queries, and change requests.

---

## ROOMNET Ticket Service

If your query or incident report is affecting multiple stations, rooms, or guests and your service is moderately impacted, please raise a ticket via the <a href="https://support.roomnet.com" target="_blank">Roomnet Support Portal</a>

Please follow the guidelines below to ensure your ticket is associated with the correct priority level.

If you do not have a login for the ROOMNET Support Portal or are unable to create one, you can raise a ticket by emailing support@roomnet.com. Once your ticket is created, you will receive an invite to create an account on our ticketing portal.

## Priority Levels

| Priority Level | Description | Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| **MEDIUM / Priority 3** | Network or service is moderately impacted but most business remains functional. | Your ticket will be reviewed and responded to within 12 hours. Remedial action will be completed or planned within 48 hours. |
| **HIGH / Priority 2** | Network or service is impacted but not down. Business impact may be high. Workaround may be available. | Your ticket will be reviewed and responded to within 4 hours. Remedial action will be completed or planned within 24 hours. |
| **CRITICAL / Priority 1** | Network service is completely down and business is impacted dramatically. No known workaround. | Your ticket will be reviewed and responded to within 1 hour. Remedial action will be completed or planned within 4 hours. This service is exclusively for Priority 1 issues. Please see notes on misuse at the end of this document. |

!!! info 
    The above response guidelines are considered as a maximum threshold only. We will endeavor to respond to all matters as quickly as possible.

## Emergency Service Line

If your query or incident report is deemed Priority 1 and is affecting a large majority of your properties, stations, rooms, or guests, please contact the 3rd line support team directly via:

- **USA:** +1 (800) 480-7974
- **UK:** +44 (0)207 692 6634
- **AUS:** +61 (2) 9159 8001

!!! warning
    These numbers have been provided exclusively to expedite Priority 1 issues only. As such, the continued misreporting of issues via the emergency number may incur additional fees. Your account manager will discuss this with you before any charges are levied.
